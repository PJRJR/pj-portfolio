IDEO · Interaction Design Fellow · 2023–2024

AI Diagnosis Tool

My Role

Interaction Design Fellow

Team

UX Designers, Data Scientists, Developers

Tools

Figma, After Effects, FigJam

Duration

Sep 2023 – Mar 2024

Overview

At IDEO, I designed a conversational AI tool that helps users diagnose appliance issues through a guided chat experience. The project required designing a chatbot persona, defining conversational flows, creating UI assets, wireframes, and prototypes — all while synthesizing research insights from a distributed team of UX designers, data scientists, and developers.

Process

Our design process began with stakeholder interviews and competitive analysis to understand the appliance repair landscape. I developed chatbot persona guidelines that balanced technical accuracy with approachable, conversational language. Working in Figma, I created wireframes and interactive prototypes that mapped the diagnostic conversation flow, incorporating branching logic for different appliance types and failure modes.

I established a brand style guide and leveraged Figma's prototyping features for motion design content, ensuring the interaction felt responsive and human-centered.

Outcome

The final prototype demonstrated how conversational AI could reduce appliance repair call-center volume while providing users with faster, more accurate diagnoses. The project strengthened my skills in conversational UX, systematic design thinking, and cross-functional collaboration within a fast-paced design consultancy.
