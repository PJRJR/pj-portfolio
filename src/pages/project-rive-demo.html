About This Piece

This interactive animation was built in Rive using state machines to respond to user input in real time. Hovering triggers a transition between idle and active states, while clicking advances through a sequence of motion states. The piece demonstrates interactive motion design — moving beyond passive animation into responsive, user-driven experiences.

This kind of work sits at the intersection of motion graphics and interaction design — exactly the space I want to operate in as a creative technologist.
